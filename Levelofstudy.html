Why whatch videos?

In order to learn a subject, it is essential to understand its theory. Most of the time, when we have some difficulty with a subject, it is because some theoretical part was not fully understood, so reviewing the theory is inevitable.

When watching video lessons, you will be reviewing the theory, approaching it from other perspectives and, sometimes, you will also be delving deeper into it. In this way, you will achieve mastery over a given subject and, consequently, will have greater ease with it.

How to find reliable video classes?

To find reliable video lessons, never forget to check the source and even the way the subject is covered. For example, a video lesson from an official university channel is reliable; but a video lesson from a person explaining a subject through countless tricks is not.

The theory presented must be clear and objective. Using comparisons or parallel examples is not a bad thing, but making them rules to "understand" the subject is something to be avoided.
